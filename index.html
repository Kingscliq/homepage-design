Our Clients

Banks and FinTechs

A huge percentage of financial companies now adopt blockchain technology because it enhances peer-to-peer payment systems, better security, and low transaction fees.

By the current market dynamics, it is estimated that the use of blockchain technology by FinTechs to grow by $460 billion by 2025. As the market potential continues to grow, we also, continue to provide them with competent security analytics to mitigate losing funds to hackers.

Government

The government’s use of blockchain technology to achieve election transparency and eliminate voter fraud, as well as registration of digital assets, tax information and other services.

HCISS serves the government by providing useful security metrics and technologies and keeping these valuable data safe from malicious attacks.

Health Services

With controversies and breach of confidentiality that have trailed patients’ data and information contained in centralized files, health service providers now use smart contract technology to solve these problems.

Because smart contracts are not totally immune to malicious attacks, our SCSA is available to provide a cover. Other clients are Media and entertainment, Real Estates, Energy and Manufacturing.
